About the Artist

Amy
Obbish

Amy Obbish is a contemporary artist whose practice spans painting, mixed media, and new media. Drawing on the tension between the tactile and the ephemeral, her work investigates memory, materiality, and the architecture of perception.

Based in Chicago, Amy has exhibited widely and maintains a studio practice that embraces both traditional and experimental approaches to image-making.
