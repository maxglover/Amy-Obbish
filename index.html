About the Artist

Amy
Obbish

Amy Obbish is an abstract artist working in watercolor and mixed media. Her practice explores the unpredictable — the bloom of pigment into wet paper, the tension between control and release, the landscapes that emerge when color is allowed to find its own form.

Each piece is an investigation of fluid dynamics and organic abstraction, building worlds from the edge of intention. Her work lives in the space between observation and intuition.
